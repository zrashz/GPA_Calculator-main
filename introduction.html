How to Use the GPA Calculator

  1. Enter the name of the course.
  2. Input the credit hours for each course.
  3. Enter the grade you received for each course (e.g., A, B+, C-).
  4. Click the "Calculate GPA" button to see your GPA and your class.
  5. Add multiple courses by clicking the "Add Course" button.
